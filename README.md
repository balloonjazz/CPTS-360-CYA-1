# CPTS-360-CYA-1
Overview

This repository contains the deliverables for my Career-Aligned Experience (CYA) project. I chose to complete the Internship Applications pathway by applying to cybersecurity, IT, and infrastructure-related internships across Washington State and surrounding regions. This repo documents the internships I applied to, the supporting materials I produced, and a reflection on the technical and professional decisions made throughout the process.

Career Goals
Short-Term Goal (6–12 months)

Secure a Summer 2026 internship, preferably at the Hanford site or one of its contracted companies, in a security, IT, or infrastructure-adjacent role.

Long-Term Goal (3–5+ years)

Obtain a full-time cybersecurity role in Washington State, ideally focusing on defensive security, SOC operations, or security engineering.
